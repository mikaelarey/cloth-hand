Who We Are?

Welcome to clothhand, your number one source for fashion. We're dedicated of giving you the very best of clothing products, with a focus on dependability, customer service, uniqueness. Founded in 2022 by Camilia Grandiol, clothhand has come a long way from its beginnings in a garage. When Camilia Grandiol first started out, her passion for sewing, providing the best quality of clothes, drove her to do more, and gave her the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the Philippines wing of the fashion and clothing industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
